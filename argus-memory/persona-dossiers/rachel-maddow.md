# Rachel Maddow / The Rachel Maddow Show — Mention Market Dossier

## Scope

This dossier covers mention markets tied to Rachel Maddow and `The Rachel Maddow Show`, especially recurring TV-show markets where live participation and show format may determine resolution before topic analysis matters.

## Known Format Trap

- Regular schedule is not enough. A Monday 9pm ET listing can still be guest-hosted, prerecorded, special programming, holiday programming, or otherwise not live.

- For live-only markets, "Rachel Maddow will not be live" can dominate all strikes. If rules require live participation, prerecorded or special programming content may send all strikes to NO or non-qualifying regardless of what the recorded segment discusses.

- Case: Memorial Day, Monday May 25, 2026. Public r/msnow programming note about 3-4 hours before air said Maddow would not be live and MS NOW would air special programming. The aired content involved a book/interview format rather than the normal live political format.

## Pre-Air Source Checklist

- Official schedule: confirm show slot and timezone, but do not treat it as live confirmation.

- Official show/MS NOW pages: check upcoming episode title, clips, guest, and special-programming language.

- Community channels 2-4h before air: check `r/msnow`, `r/RachelMaddow`, show forums, and high-signal community accounts.

- Social search: look for "Maddow tonight", "Rachel off tonight", "not live", "guest host", "special programming", "prerecorded", "Memorial Day", "holiday".

- Rules: re-open market wording if any source suggests not live, guest host, replay, or special programming.

## Analysis Rule

Do not start strike-by-strike topic analysis until live eligibility is checked. For Maddow markets, the first question is not "what topic is salient?" but "is this a qualifying live Rachel Maddow appearance?"

## Evidence Weighting

- Official network/source confirmation is strongest.

- Community manager or subreddit programming notes are C-level evidence but strong operational triggers for live-status checks.

- Fan discussion is useful for early warning, not final proof.

- Post-air clips are useful for transcript/content review, but they do not prove pre-air live eligibility unless metadata or official copy says so.
