# Argus Watchlist

Use this for recurring traps and last-mile checks that should be remembered before opening a new analysis.

## Recurring TV / Show Markets

- Rachel Maddow Mondays: check live eligibility before topic analysis. Holiday Mondays and special programming can turn a normal-looking slot into a rules/eligibility market. Last-mile sources: official schedule, MS NOW/show page, `r/msnow`, `r/RachelMaddow`, Bluesky/X searches for "not live", "off tonight", "guest host", "special programming", "prerecorded".

- TV shows with guest hosts: a show title in the schedule does not prove the named person appears or speaks. Verify required speaker.

- Sunday political shows: guest list and transcript source matter more than broad topic salience. Verify whether the market resolves against host speech, guest speech, clips, or full broadcast.

## Political Events

- Trump events: check whether the resolving content is live remarks, prepared text, pool spray, replayed clip, or quoted material. Do not transfer rally wording into ceremonial/prepared remarks without phase mapping.

- VP/White House interviews: long topic segment does not imply all second-layer words. Names, meme details, and case-specific terms usually need a direct prompt.

## Sports Announcer Markets

- Confirm broadcaster, feed, announcer assignment, game context, and whether the resolving source is national, local, radio, or alternate broadcast.

- Do not use broad sport transcript rates without broadcaster/game-state segmentation.

## Fast Last-Mile Searches

- `"not live" + show/person`
- `"off tonight" + show/person`
- `"guest host" + show/person`
- `"special programming" + network/show`
- `"prerecorded" + show/person`
- `"tonight" + exact show/person`
