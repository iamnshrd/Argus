## Why the market may be wrong

Стоп: fresh hit rate is not edge by itself.

The May 14 rally is not the same format as a polished VP interview. It has crowd work, attack lines, exaggeration, and illustrative details. `Somali`, `Learing`, `Lamborghini`, and some `Illegal Alien` usage are much more rally-coded than press-conference-coded.

The May 13 anti-fraud press conference is more format-relevant for official VP framing. In that transcript:

- `Fraud`, `California`, `Healthcare`, `Biden`, `Democrat`, `Illegal Alien`, `Minnesota` appear.
- `Somali`, `Autism`, `Learing`, `Lamborghini` do not appear.
- `China` appears only because Trump was in China, not because China is part of the fraud topic.

That is the main downgrade.
